Bible Study System - Future Feature Ideas
==========================================
Saved: March 2026

## User Engagement Features

1. Reading Plans
   - Curated journeys like "30 Days Through John" or "Understanding Grace"
   - Daily email reminders with the day's reading
   - Progress tracking with completion badges

2. Personal Notes & Highlights
   - Let users highlight verses and add private notes
   - Save favourite passages to a personal collection
   - Export notes as a PDF devotional journal

3. Audio Version
   - Text-to-speech or recorded audio for each study
   - Great for commuters, visually impaired, or those who prefer listening

## Community Features

4. Discussion Questions
   - Add reflection questions at the end of each study
   - Optional: small group discussion guide PDF download

5. Prayer Requests by Topic
   - "Struggling with anxiety? Others are praying through this too"
   - Link prayer requests to relevant Bible study topics

6. Testimonies
   - Let users share how a particular study helped them
   - "This study on forgiveness changed my marriage"

## Content Enhancement

7. Cross-References [IN PROGRESS]
   - "This passage connects to Romans 8:28" - clickable links between studies
   - "Related studies on this theme" at the bottom

8. Original Language Insights
   - Expandable sections showing Greek/Hebrew word meanings
   - "The word 'love' here is *agape*, meaning..."

9. Historical Context Boxes
   - Collapsible sections with background info
   - Maps, timelines, cultural notes

## Practical Tools

10. Verse of the Day
    - Homepage widget pulling from published studies
    - Shareable to social media

11. Print/PDF Export
    - Download a study as a beautifully formatted PDF
    - Print entire book studies as a booklet

12. Scripture Memory Cards
    - Generate flashcards from key verses in each study
    - Spaced repetition system for memorisation
