Bible Study System - Future Feature Ideas
==========================================

DAILY ENGAGEMENT
----------------
- Reading streaks: Track consecutive days of reading with visual streak counter and milestone celebrations (7 days, 30 days, etc.)
- Daily verse/devotional: A curated verse or short devotional on the homepage each day
- Push notifications: Remind users to read at their preferred time (requires PWA/service worker)
- "Continue where you left off": Prominent button on homepage to jump back into last study

GAMIFICATION & PROGRESS
-----------------------
- Achievements/badges: "Finished Genesis", "7-day streak", "Night owl reader", etc.
- Progress visualizations: Bible completion wheel/map showing which books/chapters are done
- Reading goals: Set weekly/monthly chapter targets with progress tracking
- Year-in-review: Annual stats summary (chapters read, time spent, streaks)

READING EXPERIENCE
------------------
- Audio playback: Text-to-speech or recorded audio for studies [IN PROGRESS]
- Font size/family preferences: Saved per-user
- Focus mode: Distraction-free reading with minimal UI
- Verse-by-verse mode: Step through one verse at a time with reflection prompts

SOCIAL & COMMUNITY
------------------
- Share highlights: Generate shareable images of highlighted verses
- Discussion/comments: Per-chapter discussions
- Prayer requests: Linked to specific studies
- Reading groups: Do plans together with friends/small groups

PWA/APP FEATURES
----------------
- Install as app: Add to homescreen with app icon
- Offline reading: Cache studies for offline access
- Quick actions: "Read today's chapter" shortcut
